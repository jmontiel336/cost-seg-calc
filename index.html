Cost Segregation Basics
Could you be leaving
thousands on the table?
Most property owners depreciate their entire building over 27.5–39 years. Cost segregation changes that — permanently.
🏗️

What is it?

An engineering-based tax strategy that reclassifies building components to accelerate depreciation from 27–39 years down to 5–15 years.

⚡

Bonus Depreciation

Under current tax law, reclassified components may qualify for 60% bonus depreciation — meaning massive deductions in Year 1.

🏠

Who qualifies?

Anyone who purchased, built, or renovated a property for over $250K. Residential and commercial properties both qualify.
